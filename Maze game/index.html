Welcome to our Maze Game

A maze game is a classic puzzle where the player navigates a complex network of paths and dead ends. The objective is to find a specific goal, often the exit. Players must carefully choose their steps, avoiding obstacles and traps. The challenge lies in solving the puzzle, finding the optimal path through the labyrinth.
